About COVER ART MARKET INC

COVER ART MARKET INC is a legally registered premium phone accessories company based in Etobicoke, Ontario, Canada. Founded with a passion for combining cutting-edge protection with sophisticated design, we serve customers who demand nothing but the best for their prized devices.
